## Findings

### PR-001-未修复-高-R1-R4 单样本诊断门槛可能被误用为四样本关闭证据

- **位置**: Section 4 `Workstream A: R1-R4 Fact-level Diagnostic`, especially "for at least one representative sample, and preferably all four" and Section 4 acceptance criteria.
- **问题类型**: 测试缺口 / open question 未收敛 / 不可直接实施
- **当前写法**: 计划允许 "at least one representative sample" 作为 fact-level diagnostic 目标，并在 acceptance criteria 中写 "R1-R4 can leave material release blocker only if the accepted diagnostic proves..."，但没有明确单样本诊断只能用于方法校准或 root-cause hypothesis，不得关闭全部 R1-R4。
- **反例/失败场景**: 如果 implementation/evidence worker 只诊断 `004393 / 2025`，发现主要是真实 anchor precision gap，就可能把 R1-R4 全部 reclassify 为 accepted known product behavior；但 `004194 / 2024`、`006597 / 2024`、`110020 / 2024` 的 32-39 failed facts 可能存在不同的 V2 false-positive 或 projection attachment defect pattern。
- **为什么有问题**: R1-R4 是四个不同样本，当前 accepted evidence 只证明 product-level failure counts，不证明 failure composition 同源。单样本证据无法从第一性原理推出四样本同因；这会复现项目反复禁止的 "由局部验证外推出 readiness/source truth" 问题。
- **直接证据**: Plan Section 2 lists four samples with different failed fact counts; Section 4 says "at least one representative sample, and preferably all four"; project guardrail requires root cause logic/data 同源 and release/readiness remains blocked until residuals are accepted with evidence.
- **影响**: Evidence worker 可能过早关闭 R1-R4；controller 可能把未证明样本误分类为 accepted product behavior；release residual 被错误降级，后续返工或用户可见 Evidence Confirm 语义失真。
- **建议改法和验证点**: 将 Section 4 改为两层门槛：单样本只允许建立诊断方法、分类 taxonomy 或生成 next-sample plan；关闭或重分类 R1-R4 必须覆盖全部四个 RR-S2 failing samples，或逐样本记录未覆盖样本仍 open。Acceptance criteria 要明确 "sample-specific disposition" 和 "all-four closure" 的差异。
- **修复风险（低/中/高）**: 低
- **严重程度（低/中/高/严重）**: 高
